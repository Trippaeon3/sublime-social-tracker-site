Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

Overview

Sublime Social Tracker is a private analytics tool used by authorized operators of Lady Sublime social accounts. It collects account-level metrics from connected platforms to produce local reports and trend charts.

Information Processed

When authorized by an account owner, the tracker may process platform account information such as username, display name, follower or subscriber counts, following counts, video or media counts, public profile URLs, view totals, and engagement summaries such as likes, comments, shares, reach, profile views, and website clicks.

TikTok Data

If TikTok access is authorized, TikTok data is used only to retrieve account statistics and, when enabled, video metadata for the authorized TikTok account. The tracker does not post content, send messages, or modify the TikTok account.

Storage

Metrics are stored in a local database controlled by the account operator. API credentials and access tokens are stored outside the public website and are not published in this static site.

Sharing

The tracker is intended for internal reporting. We do not sell personal information. We do not share connected platform data with advertisers or data brokers.

Retention

Historical metric snapshots may be retained to calculate growth trends over time. Authorized operators may remove stored snapshots or disconnect platform access at any time.

Revoking Access

Platform access can be revoked through the relevant platform account settings, including TikTok account authorization settings when TikTok access is connected.

Contact

For privacy or data deletion requests, use the contact options available at ladysublime.com.
