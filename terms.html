Purpose

Sublime Social Tracker is a private reporting tool for authorized operators of Lady Sublime social-media accounts. It is used to collect and review account-level metrics from connected platform APIs.

Authorized Use

Only account owners and authorized operators may connect accounts or use the tracker. Users must have permission to access any account or platform data connected to the tracker.

Platform APIs

The tracker relies on official third-party APIs. Use of connected platform data remains subject to the terms and policies of those platforms, including TikTok, Meta, YouTube, and any other connected services.

No Posting Features

The tracker is designed for reporting. It does not publish content, send direct messages, moderate comments, or modify connected social accounts.

Availability

The tracker is provided for internal operational use. API availability, permissions, and metric names may change over time, and reports may be affected by platform limits or outages.

Data Removal

Authorized operators may disconnect platform access and remove local stored snapshots when they no longer need the tracker.

For questions about these terms, use the contact options available at ladysublime.com.
